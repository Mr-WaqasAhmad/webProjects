(3) Todo App

"Welcome to our Todo App! This app allows you to create and manage your to-do lists effortlessly. Simply type in a new task in the input field and click the 'Add To List' button to add it to your list. Your tasks will be saved locally on your device, so you can access them offline anytime. You can also delete tasks by clicking the 'Delete' button next to each task. If you try to add a duplicate task, the app will alert you and prevent the duplication.
